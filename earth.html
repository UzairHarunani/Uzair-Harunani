Protecting Our Planet Starts with You


Ten Simple Things You Can Do to Help Protect the Earth

  • Reduce, reuse, and recycle. Reduce the amount of waste you produce. To preserve landfill space and natural resources, adhere to the three "R's".
  • Volunteer. Participate in community cleanups as a volunteer. You too can become involved in the preservation of your watershed.
  • Educate. By pursuing higher education, you can assist others in realizing the significance and worth of our natural resources.
  • Conserve water. Less runoff and wastewater eventually find their way into the ocean the less water you use.
  • Choose sustainable. Learn how to make smart seafood choices.
  • Shop wisely. Buy less plastic and bring a reusable shopping bag.
  • Use long-lasting light bulbs. Lightbulbs with higher energy efficiency emit fewer greenhouse gases. When you exit the area, remember that you switch off the lights as well!
  • Plant a tree. Oxygen and food are given by trees. They aid in reducing energy use, purifying the air, and halting climate change.
  • Don't send chemicals into our waterways. Choose non-toxic chemicals in the home and office.
  • Bike more. Driving causes air pollution, which is bad for the Earth.
