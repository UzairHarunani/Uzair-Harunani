 is an active competitive swimming club in Dar es Salaam, Tanzania which boasts of an ideal teaching & training environment for both children and adults. Bluefins has its own swimming pool.

Our facility is at an easily accessible location in Upanga and comprises of a clean swimming pool, changing rooms and surroundings. The surroundings offer high level of privacy in a quiet and relaxing atmosphere whether for learning or training purposes.

At Bluefins we pride ourselves in the knowledge that our Swimming program provides children with the opportunity to learn swimming in a fun, safe and positive environment. We believe that every child has the right to be safe and skilled in the water, considering the fact that our country borders the Indian Ocean on its Eastern coast and has lakes and rivers all over which almost by itself is a major reason for our children to learn water safety and other swimming skills. We also train children to become competitive swimmers & offer them the chance of participating in swimming championships & swimming galas in & outside Tanzania.

Every child will receive quality instructions based on the following teaching principles.

*Technique Focused Swimming Lessons
*Repetition, Review and Routine
*Quality and Professionalism under qualified and caring swimming instructors
*Continuous Learning
*Child Focused Facilities
*Fun through Learning and Achieving
*Communication and Feedback
*Structured Formats and Consistency
*Bluefins provides a comprehensive program of swimming lessons and water safety activities for children and adults of all ages and levels.   
*Swimming lessons are designed to teach correct and efficient    technique throughout all stages. Bluefins offers the opportunity to become familiar with the aquatic environment with a variety of fun and educational water safety activities.

All swim classes are conducted by experienced,caring and qualified instructors focusing on development in sequential and progressive stages.Bluefins provides a comprehensive swimming and water safety program.
